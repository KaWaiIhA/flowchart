🔐
Authentication
Login with 2FA, register with email verification, 3-step profile setup, then role routing.
👑
Admin Module
Manage employees, departments, leave (Step 3), attendance upload, users, and audit log.
👤
Employee Module
Dashboard, profile, leave requests (15 types), PDS, announcements, calendar, attendance + DTR.
🏢
Dept Head — Step 1
Review own dept's leave requests. Recommend → forward to HRMO, or reject. 7-day auto-reject.
🏥
HRMO — Step 2
Check leave rules compliance. Approve → Admin, reject, or return to Dept Head for clarification.
🔑
Admin Approval — Step 3
Admin reviews and approves, forwarding to Mayor. Can reject or return to HRMO.
👔
Mayor — Step 4 Final
Final authority. Approval generates official PDF with all 4 signatures and deducts leave credits.
📊
Attendance System
Upload biometrics (TXT/XLSX/XLS/CSV). Parse, calculate stats, export DTR XLSX per employee.
📄
PDS — CS Form 212
4-page official PDS: Personal, Education, Work Experience, Declarations. Download/print Legal size.
